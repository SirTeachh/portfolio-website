About Me

A young, charismatic leader that is passionate about computer sciences. Leadership has always been one of my strongest qualities and is a skill I have nurtured throughout my high school career. Not only that, a strong sense of discipline and good communication skills have carried me throughout my learning career and have always helped when it comes to conflict resolution and building healthy relationships.

The passion I have had for IT has been growing ever since I took it up as a subject in grade 10 and between then and the present I can proudly say I have developed my coding skills in the following languages: Delphi, C#, SQL and HTML. I have not only dedicated my time to solely academics but have made it a point to participate in activities that better me and my leadership skills. I have been privileged enough to be currently working as an A_STEP tutor at the University of The Free State where I am tasked with facilitating tutorials for a programming module. I am blessed enough to be doing what I love whilst assisting other people achieve their dreams.
